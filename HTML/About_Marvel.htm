  • Marvel Comics first comic book was first released in New York, during the year 1939, when it was still known as 'Timely Comics'
  • The very first comic book featured characters that we still know today, like the Human Torch, Sub-Mariner(Namor), and the Angel
  • This comic was created by Martin Goodman, but many other creators would on board to create great characters, like Jack Kirby, Stan Lee, and Steve Ditko
